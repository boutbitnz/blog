Hustle and Flow

Mar 12th, 2015 12:00 am

Week 20 below more This was an awesome week for me in class. I was able to get caught up on a lot of work. First I started with this new management software that my teacher has now set the class up with called Trello. I love it! I’m one of those people whom has to have structure, something standard right there in my face.‘signed, sealed, delivered.’

I thought having the oppertunity to be introduced to such a management system is a great for us to build skill on using that type of software since developing requires such attention, a management system is definitely needed.

I was also able to get a couple of assignments completed that were missing. For the last couple of week, a lot has gone on in my everyday environment, work as well as moving issues. I choose to take a couple days off of class to handle some family business in another state and when I got back I had fallin from being ahead to behind. Just that fast. The next week after that, I took on three more children in my house hold. With that, I was behind on work and very emotional and frustrated. I was ready to give up on a lot of people and things, however, I was not going to give up on myself with this oppertunity to build something better for my family and, along with my Husband, build stability for our future.

So I came back to class through the storm to tell you that, for the last 4-5 weeks, this last week was awesome because of realizations I’ve faced. A good tool for me when things are all over, is that I can implement wiht the problem with Critical Thinking.

I could have done none of this without my helpful classmates and my two wonderful teacher at the YWebCA; Annalise + Nathan as well. The both of them made me feel very comfortable and like I belonged here in this IT class developing I love YWebCA.
